Going through the form from top to bottom:

  • The date will automatically default to the current date but can be changed just by clicking the date itself and selecting a day from the calendar that appears.
  • The two combination boxes show a list of either Country's (left) or a list of Australian states (right). Start typing the name of the country or state, or you can select from the drop-down menu.
  • The next combination box will show a drop-down menu where you can select from a list of categories for How did you hear about us. If you want to, you can also type something that is not in the list into the box.
  • Number of people is self-explanatory.
  • Clicking 'OK' will accept the details then close the form and clicking 'CANCEL' will erase the details then close the form.
