RFDS Admin Dashboard

Built in Microsoft Access

The Home Tab


The home tab will appear whenever the dashboard is opened and is accessable from all other tabs in the dashboard
It contains 6 buttons and a handy FYI showing the total people from the day before.
The top button named 'Survey' will open a form
The next 3 buttons, named 'Passes', 'Tickets', and 'Reports' will each navigate to their respective tabs/pages.
The bottom 2 buttons name 'Feedback' and 'Help' will respectively open a form and bring you to the landing page of this website.

Reporting Problems


To report a problem, suggest a feature, or offer some feedback, click the feedback button on the home tab.
This will open a form where you can enter your name, and details of what you want to report or suggest.

Customer Surveys


Throughout the day, staff should be asking customers where they are from, and how did they hear about us.
The 'Customer Survey' button opens a form to record this data.
The form consists of:

A Pre-populated date field
This can be changed by clicking and selecting/typing a new date.
A 'Country' combo-box
To select a country if a customer is from overseas
An 'Australian States' combo-box
To select a state if the customer is visiting from within Australia
A 'How Did You Hear About Us' combo-box
Select an item from the drop down or type your own
A 'Number of People' text-box
Type the number of people you are surveying that have the same details
A 'Combo-box' can be treated as a 'Text-box' OR a 'Drop-down' box. Meaning you can select a pre-defined option, or type your own.

The Passes Tab


When the 'Passes' tab is opened, you will see 5 buttons, 2 of which ('Feedback' and 'Help') are the same as the 'Home' tab.
The 'Home' button will navigate back to the home tab.
'New' will open a form to enter details of new territorians.
'Lookup' will open a search box used to find pre-existing territorians.

New Territorian Pass

To enter a new territorian, click the 'New' button in the 'Passes' tab to open a form (see picture)


The fields are listed below along with descriptions of what should be in each field.

Names
First and last name of the territorian/s
If there are multiple people with the same last name and subsequent contact details, enter the names like so:
"John & Betty & Paul - SMITH" or
"John/Betty/Paul SMITH"
Admission
A combo-box to choose the type of admission of the territorian. If multiple people are on the pass, just choose the majority admission.
Price
The price that was paid for the admission type. E.g. Admission: "Adult" Price: "$30"
The 'Price' field on this form will update automatically to reflect the correct price when the 'Admission' field is changed.
Mobile
Enter the mobile number of the territorian. This can be used later to lookup a territorian.
Email
Enter the email address of the territorian. This is for marketing purposes (if they agree).
Address
The territorians address to prove that they are indeed a local.
Created
This is the date that the pass was created and should already be pre-filled. All passes are valid for 12 months and this field is used to calculate the expiry date.
Visits
This field displays the amount of times the terriotrian has returned to the facility.
